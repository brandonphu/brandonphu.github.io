ECE FYDP - AutoGlide

AutoGlide is an inexpensive add-on to aftermarket automobiles designed to improve vehicle control and comfort through an advanced suspension system. This device improves traditional suspension by giving the user direct control of a carâ€™s coilover stiffness without having to lift the hood. Using GPS, accelerometers, stepper motors, and intelligent control algorithms, AutoGlide allows the driver to directly control the suspension settings or automatically adjust them by analysing the road conditions for optimal performance. Either by lowering the stiffness on bumpier roads or tightening it for better handling on flat stretches, this project provides a more comfortable and performance-oriented ride.
